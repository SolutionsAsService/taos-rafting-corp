Trips & Packages

Explore the Rio Grande with hand-crafted rafting experiences for all skill levels and adventure styles.

Half-Day Whitewater

Perfect for first-timers or families, this 3-hour run through the Lower Gorge offers splashes, fun rapids, and stunning views. Daily departures.

Full-Day Racecourse

Challenge yourself with Class III rapids on the iconic Racecourse stretch. Includes riverside lunch and expert guides. Great for small groups!

Scenic Float

Drift peacefully down the mellow stretches of the river, perfect for photographers, bird-watchers, and nature lovers. Minimal paddling required.

Custom Private Trips

Want to create your own experience? Book a private guide and boat for a fully tailored adventure. Half or full day options available.
