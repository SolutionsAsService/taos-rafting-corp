About Us

Born from the river. Raised by the canyon. We live for water, wilderness, and bringing people together outside.

Our Roots

Founded in 1998 by locals and lifelong river runners, Taos River Adventures started as a two-boat operation and has grown into one of New Mexico’s top outdoor outfitters.

Our Mission

We’re here to share the magic of the Rio Grande. Our trips are guided by a deep respect for nature, Indigenous lands, and the joy of safe, authentic adventure.

Meet the Team

Our guides are certified, passionate, and fun — and many have been with us for over a decade. Whether you’re paddling hard or floating easy, they’ve got your back.
